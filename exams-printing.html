Flyers Printing Pricing Factors

Here’s what affects the final cost of your flyer order:

  • Flyer Size – A6, A5, DL, or custom sizes
  • Quantity – The more you print, the lower the cost per flyer (bulk discounts apply)
  • Paper Type – Glossy, matte, silk, or card stock
  • Single or Double-Sided – Double-sided flyers cost slightly more
  • Finishing – Laminated, folded, UV-coated, or die-cut
  • Design Services – Optional add-on if you need us to design your flyer
