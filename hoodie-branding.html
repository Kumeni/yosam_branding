Here’s a closer look at our hoodie printing methods:

Heat Transfer / Vinyl Printing

For bold, sharp designs like names, numbers, or logos, heat transfer (vinyl printing) is a fantastic option. This technique uses heat to bond the design to the fabric, giving you a crisp, clean look that stands out. It’s also a great choice if you’re printing small batches of hoodies or need personalized details for each piece.

Best for: Sports hoodies, event wear, and personalized hoodies with names or numbers.

Learn More... Contact Us

Embroidery

Want a more professional, high-end finish? Embroidery adds a stylish, stitched look to your hoodie. Unlike regular prints, embroidery gives your hoodie a premium appearance that’s perfect for corporate branding or creating a sophisticated fashion piece. The threads are durable, so your design stays intact for years.

Best for: Corporate hoodies, uniforms, and premium custom designs.

Screen Printing

If you’re looking for vibrant colors and long-lasting results, screen printing is the way to go. It’s the most popular option for bulk hoodie printing because it delivers bold, eye-catching designs that hold up wash after wash. Whether it’s your company logo, a team name, or a creative artwork, screen printing ensures your design pops.
