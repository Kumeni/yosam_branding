TYPES OF LOGOS

Emblem

Consists of woodmark, lettermark or logomark within a shape that is essential to the design.

Woodmark

Comprises a stand-alone word or words i.e Cocacola, FedEx.

Lettermark

Contains one letter or an abbreviation i.e EA.

Logomark

Contains a symbol only.

Combination

Consists of Logomark + woodmark or Lettermark + woodmark.
